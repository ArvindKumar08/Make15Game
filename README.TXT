This game was developed by me during my studies in Northumbria University London.
This game is against an easy level of computer

Rules of the game:
In this game there are 9 numbers, ranging from 1 to 9. You and the computer take turns to pick up a number, when 3 of a player's picks can add up to 15, then that player wins.

Description
Choose 3 numbers that add up to 15 to win.
